About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Bar-Ilan University and a researcher at the Bar-Ilan Center for Research in Applied Cryptography and Cyber Security.


My research focuses on theoretical computer science and cryptography. I am particularly interested in interactive proof systems (such as SNARGs and IOPs) and their applications to verifiable computation.


I completed my PhD at the Weizmann Institute of Science, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Moni Naor.
